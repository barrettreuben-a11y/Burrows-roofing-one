About Us

A Trusted Name Since 1971

Burrows Roof Cleaning Services Limited has been looking after Bay of Plenty homes for over 30 years. We're a local business based in Waihi Beach — we know the climate, the roofing materials and the damp conditions that let moss and lichen take hold.
